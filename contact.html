FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to change my website?
No. We embed forms directly on your existing site. If you'd prefer, we can also build a standalone landing page — but a full website redesign is not required or included.
What platform does this run on?
HSP systems run on a managed white-label platform (built on GoHighLevel). You get your own sub-account, your own login, and full access to your data — we manage the infrastructure.
What does the monthly platform fee cover?
Platform access — your CRM, pipeline, automations, forms, phone number, text messaging, and email sending. It keeps everything running. Think of it as your operational infrastructure subscription. Pricing is discussed on your scoping call.
Do I need a retainer?
No. Retainers are optional. The system is built to run on its own. Retainers are for businesses that want ongoing optimization, new workflow builds, or a dedicated support contact.
Can I add modules later?
Yes — that's the whole point. Start with the Lead Response System. Add Reviews, IVR, Hiring, Quoting, or Internal Ops whenever your business is ready.
What if I already have a CRM?
We can discuss migration or integration. In most cases, businesses that come to us have a CRM that isn't set up properly — or one that's just a contact list with no automation behind it. We replace the chaos with a real system.
Do you do marketing / ads / SEO?
No. We build operational infrastructure — the system that handles leads after they come in. If you need more leads, we can recommend partners. Our job is making sure you never waste the leads you already get.
What if it doesn't work for my business?
That's why we review your setup before the call. If we don't think we can help, we'll tell you. We only take on businesses where the system makes sense.
