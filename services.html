What this actually solves

Most service businesses lose leads because nobody responds fast enough. You're on a job site, your phone rings, nobody picks up, and the prospect calls your competitor. Or they fill out a form on your website and hear nothing for 3 days.

The Lead Response System eliminates this entirely. Every form submission triggers an instant text and email. Every missed call gets an automatic text-back. Every lead enters your pipeline with the right tags, assignments, and notifications so your team knows exactly what to do next.

What's included — in detail

  • Multi-step intake forms that qualify leads before they hit your pipeline — service type, property details, timeline, photos
  • Instant acknowledgment via text and email the moment a form is submitted — under 2 minutes, every time
  • Missed-call text-back that sends a pre-written SMS if you can't answer the phone
  • CRM pipeline with stages mapped to your sales process — new lead, contacted, estimate scheduled, quote sent, won/lost
  • Automated follow-up sequence that nudges leads who haven't responded
  • Review request workflow triggered after a job is marked complete
  • Team notifications and routing so the right person sees the right lead immediately
